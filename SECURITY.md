## Reporting a Vulnerability

To report a security vulnerability, please email security@yourprojectdomain.com with a detailed description of the issue, including steps to reproduce and any relevant logs or screenshots.

We will acknowledge receipt of your report within 2 business days. You can expect updates on the status of your report at least once per week.
If the vulnerability is confirmed, we will work to release a fix as soon as possible and credit you in the release notes (unless you request otherwise). If the report is declined, we will provide a clear explanation.
